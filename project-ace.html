Acing the Undergrad

Acing the Undergrad: Quiz Game is a turn-based mobile game that covers the topics in the Acing the Undergrad course for college success. The player takes the role of a student who, with the help of their mentors, answer course questions to defeat enemies relating to the corresponding topic. The player can also customize their character with 3 skin tones and 4 hair styles to choose from.

This mobile game was developed by a group of 5 individuals for the college-preperation company Acing the Undergrad. The game was released for free on the Play Store and Itch.io. The team consisted of a sound designer, 2 artists, a project manager, and myself as the programmer. Since I was the only programmer, I was responsbile for all of the game's functionality including the turn-based battle system, character creator, world map movement, and saving.
