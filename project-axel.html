Axel Smooth
Saves Charlotte

Role

Mechanics Programmer

Timeline

January 2018 - Present

Team

Team X

Axel Smooth is a 1-2 player beat em' up arcade game where Charlotte has been invaded by aliens, a task that Axel Smooth must tackle. The game takes place in the Charlotte area, featuring including North Davidson, Uptown, and Abari, the arcade bar the game will be featured in. Both with Axel's fists and bats, the player will beat up several types of alien creatures in addition to 2 alien bosses.

This game was developed by a group of 11 individuals from Central Piedmont, consisting of 4 artists, 2 programmers (including me), 2 project managers, 2 level designers, and a sound designer. The game was featured at two of Abari's aniversary parties and recieved very positive feedback. Additionally, it was featured in an article by the Queen City Nerve, a Charlotte newspaper
